Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What is your turnaround time?

We offer flexible delivery times: 48 hours (Basic), 24 hours (Standard), or 12 hours (Premium). Rush orders are possible - contact us for details.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes! Basic plan includes 1 revision, Standard includes 3 revisions, and Premium includes unlimited revisions. We want you to be 100% satisfied.

What file formats do you work with?

We accept MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV, and more for video. For graphics: PSD, PNG, JPG, SVG. We'll deliver in your preferred format.

Can you work with my brand guidelines?

Absolutely! We love working with established brand guidelines. Just share them with us and we'll ensure all work matches your brand identity perfectly.

How do I pay?

We accept bank transfer, credit card, PayPal, and Apple Pay. Payment is required upfront, but we offer a money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied.

Do you provide source files?

Yes! With Premium plan, you get all source files (PSD, AEP, project files). With Standard and Basic, source files are available for an additional fee.

Can you handle confidential projects?

Definitely! We sign NDAs (Non-Disclosure Agreements) for sensitive projects. Your privacy and confidentiality are our top priorities.
